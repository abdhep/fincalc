Frequently Asked Questions

💰 Are the calculators free? ▼

Yes! All our calculators are completely free to use. No subscriptions, no payments, no hidden charges. Use them as much as you want, forever.

🔒 Is my data stored on your servers? ▼

No. All calculations happen in your browser. Your financial data never leaves your device. We have no access to your information — complete privacy guaranteed.

📱 Can I use them offline? ▼

Yes. Once the calculator page is loaded in your browser, it works offline. You can save the HTML file locally and use it without internet access.

💾 Can I save my calculations? ▼

Yes. Personal Finance, Loan, and PPF calculators allow you to save and load your data as local files. Your files stay on your device for complete privacy.

🆘 What if I need help? ▼

We provide email support for all our calculators. Contact us with any questions, and we'll help you get the most out of your calculator.

📊 Do the Quick Calculators save data? ▼

Quick Calculators are designed for instant calculations and don't have save/load functionality. They're perfect for one-time calculations without the need for data persistence.
