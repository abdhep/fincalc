📊 What information we collect

🚫 We do NOT collect

  • Your income or salary
  • Your savings or investment amounts
  • Your liabilities or debts
  • Your retirement or goal figures
  • Any data you enter into any calculator
  • Your name, unless you contact us

✅ We may passively collect

  • Pages visited and time of visit
  • Browser type and operating system
  • General geographic region (country/state)
  • Referring website
  • Whether you are a returning visitor

Passively collected data relates to your device or browser — not to you personally. It is used only to understand how our site is used and to improve it.

🚫 Calculator data — what we do NOT collect

This deserves its own section because it is the most important privacy matter for our users.

When you use any calculator on ArthaStambh.com, every figure you enter — your age, income, expenses, savings, investments, liabilities, retirement targets, and financial goals — is processed entirely within your browser. This data is never sent to our servers, never stored in a database, and never accessible to us in any form.

When you use the Save/Load feature in our Personal Finance Calculator, your data is saved as a file on your own device. We do not receive, see, or store this file.

This is not merely a policy statement — it is a technical fact of how our calculators are built.

📧 Information you send us

If you contact us via the Contact Us form or by email, we collect the information you voluntarily provide (such as your name, email address, and message). This information is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or query
  • Follow up on your request if needed

We do not use contact form submissions for unsolicited marketing. We do not sell or share your contact details with third parties.

🔧 How we use information

Passively collected browsing information is used to:

  • Understand which pages and calculators are most useful
  • Improve navigation, performance, and content
  • Diagnose technical problems

We do not use this information to identify individual users, and we do not sell any information to third parties.

🍪 Cookies and analytics

Our website may use standard analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) that place small cookies on your browser to help us understand aggregate usage patterns. These cookies do not identify you personally.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not affect your ability to use any of our calculators, as calculator functionality does not depend on cookies.

🔓 Disclosure of information

ArthaStambh.com will not disclose any personal information we hold about you to third parties, except:

  • Where required to comply with applicable law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of ArthaStambh.com, its users, or the public
  • To technology service providers who assist in operating and maintaining the website — under strict confidentiality obligations

We will never sell your personal information to any party for any purpose.

🔐 Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information on our website. Since our calculators do not transmit financial data, the risk of sensitive financial information being intercepted or compromised is eliminated by design.

For contact form submissions and email correspondence, standard email and web security practices apply.

✋ Your rights

Since our calculators do not collect or store your financial data, there is no personal financial information held by us that could be subject to an access or deletion request.

If you have contacted us and wish to know what information we hold about you, or wish to have it corrected or deleted, please email us at [email protected].

📝 Amendments

ArthaStambh.com reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Our core commitment — that calculator data never leaves your device — will not be changed without prominent notice to users.
